Main scripts:
-------------

- silverbox_train_lin.py: train a linear state-space model on the silverbox dataset
- silverbox_train_poly.py: train a polynomial state-space model on the silverbox dataset. Results seem to be in line with [1]
- silverbox_test.py: test the polynomial state-space model and compute metrics

[1] J. Paduart, L. Lauwers, J. Swevers, K. Smolders, J. Schoukens, and R. Pintelon. Identification of the Silverbox Benchmark Using Nonlinear State-Space Models.
